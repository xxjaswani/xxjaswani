Who is Xxjaswani?

Xxjaswani is a music producer who specializes in making Lo-Fi Beats with many elements sourced from live instruments and old funk, soul, and Jazz Samples. The Indian debuted his first Mixtape ‘Echoes of Silence’ back in 2021, and has continued to produce songs with elements that can create a calming atmosphere. With releases like ‘travel.zip’ and ’social distancing’, and the producer’s latest album ‘toronto’. Xxjaswani’s music has been released independently on all digital streaming platforms, with the exception of a few label releases issued by Chill Moon Music and Modern Station.
