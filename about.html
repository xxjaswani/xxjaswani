BIO

Xxjaswani is a young Lofi Beat Maker from India. Jaswani has had a keen interest in music and art when he started producing music at the age of 11. He specializes in making Lofi beats with elements sourced from samples and live instruments. He made his debut in 2020 with single releases such as Let Go, State Of Mind, dreams, dusk, and christmas. He later refined his approach through a collection of EP and Album length releases such as travel.zip, social distancing, and his debut album echoes of silence. His output has been self-issued digitally and made available on all streaming platforms. His newest album toronto was recently released, and its leading singles have received massive streaming numbers in the thousands. The album is mainly comprised of simplistic beats that create a feeling of isolation, happiness, and serenity. The album also features the singer and songwriter ZAY NAB on track number 3.
